Common Questions

Lock Rekeying FAQ

Rekeying changes the internal pins inside your lock cylinder so old keys no longer work. A locksmith replaces the pin tumblers to match a new key — same security as a new lock, at a fraction of the cost.
Rekeying is almost always cheaper. Rekeying typically costs $25–$40 per lock in Waterloo Region, while replacing a quality deadbolt costs $80–$200+ per lock plus labour. If your locks are in good condition, rekeying is the smart choice.
Rekey when: moving into a new home, after losing keys, after a roommate moves out, after a break-in, or when you've given out spare keys and want full control back. Rekeying is recommended for every new homeowner in Waterloo Region.
Most homes in Kitchener, Waterloo, and Cambridge can be fully rekeyed in 30–60 minutes. We bring all equipment on-site and rekey while you wait — no need to leave your home or wait days for parts.
